================================================================================
WHY THIS MAKES SENSE
================================================================================

Third Party (TP) Insurance:
---------------------------
- Covers damage to OTHER people/vehicles
- Risk is based on potential damage vehicle can cause
- Larger engines → higher speeds → higher risk
- Age does NOT affect third-party damage potential
- A 10-year-old car can cause same damage as new car

Own Damage (OD) Insurance (for comparison):
-------------------------------------------
- Covers YOUR OWN vehicle
- Risk is based on vehicle's current value
- Age DOES affect value (depreciation)
- Hence OD rates vary by age, but TP rates don't
